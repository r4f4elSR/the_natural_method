## SOLUTION (səˈluʃən)
Who is the man? **He is the father**. Who is the woman? **She is the mother**. Who is the boy? **He is the son**. Who are the two girls? **They are the daughters**. Who are the two girls? **They are Helen and the baby**. Who are the parents of John, Helen, and the baby? **They are Mr. and Mrs. Smith, the father and the mother**. How many persons are there in the family? **There are five persons**. Who are the five persons? **They are the father, the mother, the son, and the two daughters**. How many children are there in the family? **There are three children**. Who are they? T**hey are John, Helen, and the baby**. How many girls are there in the family? **There are two girls**. Who is Helen? **She is the daughter**. Who is Mrs. Smith? **She is the mother**. Who is Mr. Smith? **He is the father**.
